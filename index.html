Get To Know The Hollow Sparrows!

The Hollow Sparrows are an indie folk band formed in Ireland in 2016. The group consists of Finn Wilder (27), Eloise Harper (27), Lila Hayes (26), Tobin Moon (26) and Amara Finch (27). The members of the band first met in their elementry band class back in Westport and have continued playing together ever since. They enjoy sharing stories about their childhood and growing up together through their music and songs.

Latest Album

"Wings In The Wind," Hollow Sparrows latest released album came out on November 30, 2024.
